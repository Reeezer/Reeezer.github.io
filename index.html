Personnalized Spinal Cord Reconstruction @ .NeuroRestore

Spring - Fall 2024

Built a deep learning pipeline to support neuroprosthetic planning for Spinal Cord Injury (SCI) patients through personalized spinal cord reconstructions from MRI data. Pre-trained a 3D Masked Autoencoder based on Vision Transformers (ViT) to capture the structural "language" of the spinal cord, enhancing downstream tasks like segmentation and artefact removal with limited labeled data. Fine-tuned nnU-Net and HarDNet architectures for spinal root segmentation, addressing challenges such as anatomical variability and tightly packed structures. Improved pre-annotation quality through advanced pre-processing, augmentation, and post-processing, demonstrating how AI can streamline clinical workflows and reduce manual annotation effort.

Deep Learning Neuroimaging Fine-Tuning Research

Based on META Masked Autoencoders, nnUNet and HarDNet
